2. Privacy Policy for the Rectly iOS App

2.1 Principle: Local Data Storage

Rectly was developed with a focus on privacy. All your recordings, transcripts, notes, and personal data are stored exclusively locally on your iOS device by default. We have no access to your data and it does not leave your device – unless you explicitly choose to use external services.

2.2 Data Processing in Detail

2.2.1 Audio Recordings

  • Audio files are stored in M4A format in the Documents folder of your app
  • These files remain on your device and are not uploaded
  • You have full control over your recordings at all times (delete, export)
  • With iCloud backup enabled, audio files can be backed up to your iCloud

2.2.2 Transcription Modes

Local Mode (Apple Speech Recognition):

  • Transcription is performed entirely on your device using Apple Speech Recognition
  • No data transmission to external servers
  • No API costs
  • All AI features are disabled in this mode

API Mode (OpenAI Whisper / AssemblyAI):

  • When you choose API mode, audio files are sent to external services
  • You must provide your own API keys for these services
  • Transmission is encrypted (HTTPS)
  • External providers:
    • OpenAI (Whisper): Privacy policy at openai.com/privacy
    • AssemblyAI: Privacy policy at assemblyai.com/legal/privacy-policy

2.2.3 AI Features (API Mode Only)

  • AI summaries are generated via OpenAI Chat API
  • For this, the transcript is sent to OpenAI
  • You need your own OpenAI API key
  • OpenAI's privacy policies apply to this processing

2.2.4 API Keys

  • Your API keys are stored encrypted in the iOS Keychain
  • Access is via biometric authentication (Face ID / Touch ID)
  • API keys never leave your device
  • We as app developers have no access to your keys

2.3 Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Microphone: For recording audio (required)
  • Speech Recognition: For local transcription (optional)
  • Notifications: For alerts when transcription is complete (optional)

2.4 No Analytics or Tracking

Rectly does not contain any analytics tools, tracking software, or advertising. We do not collect usage data and do not create user profiles.

2.5 Export and Sharing

You can export transcripts and recordings at any time via the iOS share function. You decide where the data is sent (email, cloud services, etc.). For these external services, their own privacy policies apply.

3. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data
  • Correction of incorrect data
  • Deletion of your data
  • Restriction of data processing
  • Data portability
  • Object to data processing

Since app data is stored locally on your device, you can exercise your rights directly by deleting data in the app or by uninstalling it.

4. Data Security

We use technical and organizational security measures to protect your data against accidental or intentional manipulation, loss, destruction, or access by unauthorized persons.

5. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to occasionally adapt this Privacy Policy so that it always complies with current legal requirements or to implement changes to our services. The new Privacy Policy will then apply to your next visit.
